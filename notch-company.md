# Notch — Company Overview

## What They Do
Notch builds **production-grade AI agents for customer support** — not chatbots.
Their agents work **shoulder-to-shoulder with human support agents** inside enterprise customer support organizations.

Key differentiators:
- Agents are registered inside the org's customer support system as full agents (not a bolt-on)
- Can perform **real actions**: refunds, policy lookups, case resolution — not just answering questions
- Handles **sensitive, high-regulation industries**: insurance, banking, financial services
- Strong **guardrails** to prevent the agent from doing anything unauthorized

## How They Handle Trust
- They don't just ship and pray — they run extensive simulation before going live
- Run in "shadow mode" first: makes all decisions but doesn't execute, until accuracy is high enough
- Trust is **calibrated over time**, not granted once
- Post-release: continuous monitoring, improvement, and audit trail
- The goal: reach a precision level where fully autonomous execution is safe

## Clients / Use Cases
- **Phoenix Group** (Menora insurance, Excellence investment portfolio management, claims handling)
- Financial transactions, insurance claims — real money moving autonomously
